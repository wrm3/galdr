# Pack: content-creation

Social strategy, video scripting, storyboarding, and platform ad specifications for content creators and marketers.

## What This Installs

- `social-media-marketing` — YouTube/TikTok/Instagram/LinkedIn growth strategy, thumbnails, influencers
- `storyboard-creation` — Shot composition, camera angles, movement, continuity; pre-production workflow
- `explainer-video` — AIDA script formulas, pacing rules, scene planning, voiceover + music guidance
- `video-ad-specs` — TikTok/Instagram/YouTube/Facebook/LinkedIn dimensions, timing, and AIDA framework

## Prerequisites

None (documentation skill — no executables needed)

## Install

```powershell
.\skill_packs\content-creation\install.ps1
.\skill_packs\content-creation\install.ps1 -ProjectRoot "C:\my-project"
```

## Uninstall

Delete the installed skill directories from your project's `.cursor/skills/`, `.agent/skills/`, etc.
