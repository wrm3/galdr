## Stock Option Types

| Type | Tax at Grant | Tax at Exercise | Tax at Sale |
|------|--------------|-----------------|-------------|
| **ISO** | None | AMT may apply | LTCG if held 2yr/1yr |
| **NSO** | None | Ordinary income | STCG or LTCG |
| **RSU** | None | Ordinary income at vest | STCG or LTCG |

### ISO vs NSO Decision

- **ISOs**: Tax-advantaged for employees; $100K/year exercise limit; must be employees (not contractors)
- **NSOs**: No exercise limit; available to contractors and advisors; company gets tax deduction at exercise

## 83(b) Election

File within **30 days** of restricted stock grant to be taxed at grant-date value instead of vesting-date value.

**When to file**: Early-stage grants where current FMV is low and expected to appreciate significantly.

**Risk**: If you leave before vesting, you've paid tax on stock you don't own.

## Standard Vesting Schedule

```
4-year vesting with 1-year cliff:

Year 0-1: 0% vested (cliff period)
Year 1:   25% vests on anniversary
Year 1-4: Remaining 75% vests monthly (1/48 per month)
Year 4:   100% vested
```

## Total Compensation Framework

```
Total Comp = Base Salary + Equity Value + Benefits + Bonus

Example (Series A Senior Engineer):
  Base:    $180,000
  Equity:  0.20% over 4 years (~$50K/yr at current valuation)
  Benefits: ~$15,000/yr (health, 401k match, etc.)
  Bonus:   10% target ($18,000)
  ─────────────────────
  Total:   ~$263,000/yr
```
